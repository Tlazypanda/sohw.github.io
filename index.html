Spread Open Healthy Web

This project comes under Web Literacy


Spread Open Healthy Web (SOHW) is an open project seeks to bring the culture of openness among people, to come forward and share their technical skills with diverse set of people. The project is designed to guide people who joined the web with keen interest to update themselves towards the current trend by learning or to develop something for the betterment of the web.


SOHW is a project being developed under the Mozilla Open Leadership Program, Round 5 and mentored by Pranshu Khanna.
